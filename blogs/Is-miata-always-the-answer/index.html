Is miata always the answer?

yes, of course it is!

The Miata is a great car for people who love driving. Why? It’s lightweight, has rear-wheel drive, and is really fun to drive around curves. It’s also cheap to buy, doesn’t cost much to maintain, and is very reliable, which makes it perfect for people who want to enjoy driving without spending too much money.

You can also customize the Miata in many ways. You can add parts to improve its performance or change its looks to make it unique. Plus, there is a big, friendly community of Miata owners where you can get advice and find parts.

So, if you’re looking for a fun, reliable, and customizable sports car, the Miata is a great choice.
